# Cybersecurity Learning Series — Week 1

## Introduction to Cybersecurity

### Topic
What is Cybersecurity and why does it exist?

Before learning hacking techniques, tools, or vulnerabilities, there is one important question:

Why did cybersecurity even become necessary?

### The Digital World Today

We use the internet for almost everything:

- Online banking
- Social media
- Business operations
- Cloud storage
- Personal communication

This means a huge amount of sensitive data now lives in digital systems.

And wherever valuable data exists, attackers will always try to access it.

### Common Cyber Threats

Over the years, organizations started facing problems like:

- Data breaches
- Financial fraud
- Identity theft
- Ransomware attacks
- System disruptions

This is exactly where cybersecurity comes in.

### What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage.

Its goal is simple:

To keep digital systems **secure, reliable, and trusted.**

In today’s digital world, cybersecurity is no longer optional.

**It is a necessity.**

---

🛡 Because in cybersecurity, it's not a question of **if** an attack will happen — but **when.**
